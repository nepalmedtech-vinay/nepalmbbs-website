Honest Comparison
Why Nepal MBBS — Facts Only
No exaggeration. These are factual advantages supported by official sources.

✈️ No Visa for Indians

India-Nepal open border agreement. Indian citizens enter with valid passport or Aadhaar card — no visa process, no embassy visit.

🏛️ NMC India Recognised

Select colleges are recognised by NMC India. Always verify on nmc.org.in before applying.

📚 Indian-Aligned Curriculum

Nepal MBBS syllabus closely mirrors Indian MBBS — same subjects, similar exam pattern, which aids FMGE/NExT preparation.

🗣️ English Medium, Hindi Understood

Medium of instruction is English. Hindi is widely spoken and understood — no language barrier for Indian students.

🍛 Familiar Food & Culture

Indian food is available in most college hostels. Cultural similarities make daily life comfortable.

🏥 Clinical Exposure

Nepal medical colleges have attached teaching hospitals. Disease patterns similar to India — relevant clinical training from early years.

Common Questions
Parents & Students — Top Questions Answered
Admission & Eligibility
Is NEET mandatory for Nepal MBBS?
+

Yes. As per NMC India guidelines, NEET qualification is mandatory for Indian students who wish to practice medicine in India after MBBS abroad. Minimum 50th percentile (General). Verify at nmc.org.in.

Is a Nepal MBBS degree valid in India?
+

Valid only from NMC India recognised colleges. You must clear FMGE or NExT (by NBE) to practice in India. Always check: NMC College Search.

What is the duration of MBBS in Nepal?
+

5.5 years — 4.5 years academic + 1 year compulsory internship. Same structure as MBBS in India. Source: mec.gov.np.

When do Nepal MBBS admissions open?
+

Typically July–October. MEC publishes official notices. Track at: entrance.mec.gov.np.

Fees & Finance
How much does Nepal MBBS cost?
+

Fees are regulated by MEC Nepal and vary by college. Contact us for the current official fee structure. Verify at mec.gov.np.

Is an education loan available for Nepal MBBS?
+

Indian banks (SBI, Bank of Baroda, etc.) provide education loans for MBBS at NMC recognised colleges. NMC recognition letter required. Consult your bank directly for terms.

Career & Future
What is FMGE / NExT and is it difficult?
+

FMGE is conducted by NBE twice yearly. NExT is replacing FMGE. Nepal's aligned curriculum helps, but serious preparation is required. Info: natboard.edu.in.

Can I do NEET PG in India after Nepal MBBS?
+

After FMGE/NExT + 1-year India internship, you may be eligible for NEET PG. Rules are subject to NMC updates — verify at nmc.org.in.

Life & Safety
Do Indian students need a visa for Nepal?
+

No. Indian citizens do not require a visa for Nepal under the bilateral open border agreement. Valid Indian passport or government-issued photo ID is sufficient.

Is Nepal safe for Indian students?
+

Nepal and India share historically close ties. Top colleges provide campus security and separate hostels. We recommend speaking with current students before deciding.

Life in Nepal
What to Expect — Honest Picture
🌡️

Weather

Kathmandu: 5–30°C. Pleasant in spring and autumn. Cold winters (5–10°C). Monsoon June–August.

🍛

Food

Indian food in most college hostels. Dal, roti, sabzi readily available. Indian restaurants in Kathmandu and Pokhara.

🏠

Hostel

On-campus hostels with separate blocks for male and female students. 24/7 security, Wi-Fi, study halls.

💰

Monthly Cost

Approx ₹15,000–25,000/month for hostel, food, transport, personal. Varies by lifestyle and location.

📱

Connectivity

Indian SIM cards roam in Nepal. Affordable local SIMs available. Good internet infrastructure in major cities.

🎓

Student Community

Large Indian student community in Nepal colleges. Cultural events, student associations, peer support available.
